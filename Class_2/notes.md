# Reading 2 - Simon Sinek, Start with Why: How Great Leaders Inspire Everyone to Take Action, Penguin, 2011, Chapter 3 (“The Golden Circle”)

## basics
- Conventional Leaders/Companies: use the **"WHAT -> HOW -> WHY"** structure to motivate people --> which is **manipulative**
- Inspirational Leaders/Companies: use the **"WHY -> HOW -> WHAT"** structure to motivate people --> which is **inspirational**


### Individual Exercise: choose something and propose a WHAT-HOW-WHY && WHY-HOW-WHAT way

Situation: Proposing Delivery Apps:

- WHAT-HOW-WHY
    - **WHAT -->** We are a delivery app which delivers food so that you don't need to visit the store.  
    - **HOW -->** We have delivery personnel involved and a tie-up with the restaurants that prioritize your order or at least have a different queue for you.  
    - **WHY -->** We are doing this so your wait times reduce and all restaurants become more accessible.  

- WHY-HOW-WHAT 
    - **WHY -->** we are doing this bcoz:
        - less hassle
        - more variety
        - getting door-step is better MIGHT actually be better in some cases THAN traveling to eat is not the best way

    - **HOW & WHAT -->** simple application on your phone - we handle the rest
